Who We Are

POWERING THE BAY AREA SINCE DAY ONE

We started Witness Electrical LLC doing residential work in the Bay Area — panel upgrades, rewires, EV chargers, lighting. Over a decade later we're still doing that, and we've expanded into something most local electricians don't touch: industrial transformer distribution.

We're working directly with manufacturers in Mexico to supply transformers to distributors and end-users across California. Any type. Up to 5,000 KVA. Competitive pricing, real lead times, and people you can actually talk to.

Both sides run on the same principle: show up, do the work right, don't make it complicated.
